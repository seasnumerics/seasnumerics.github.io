Numerics Journal Club at Harvard

Information

We meet weekly on Fridays, 9:30am - 11:00am, at 52 Oxford St, room B166. The goal of the reading group is to read classic papers in numerical analysis, fast methods, and their applications. We hope to roughly follow the structure described by Nick Trefethen here. If you'd like to present, please sign up. On weeks where no one is presenting, we will read a classic paper and have an informal discussion about it.
